# KPI Brief — Levant Tech Solutions

## KPI 1: Department Salary Expenditure

**Definition:** Total annual salary cost for each department, measured by the sum of salaries for all employees in the department.
**Current value:** Engineering has the highest total salary cost at $938,000.00.
**Interpretation:** Engineering is the largest salary expenditure area, so it is a key focus for cost optimization and resource planning.

## KPI 2: Hiring Momentum

**Definition:** Number of new hires by year, measured by employee hire dates grouped by calendar year.
**Current value:** 16 hires in 2024, compared to 24 hires in 2023 and 14 hires in 2022.
**Interpretation:** Hiring activity peaked in 2023 and has slowed through 2024 and 2025, indicating a possible stabilization in workforce growth or a shift to optimizing existing staff.

## KPI 3: Unassigned Employee Count

**Definition:** Number of employees without any project assignment, measured by employees who do not appear in the project_assignments table.
**Current value:** 18 unassigned employees.
**Interpretation:** A sizeable portion of the workforce is not currently assigned to projects, signaling an opportunity to improve employee utilization and project staffing alignment.
